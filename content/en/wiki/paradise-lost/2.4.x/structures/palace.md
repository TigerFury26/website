## Description
Palaces are rare, floating structures, with bases that appear to dissolve away underneath them.

This structure is very quiet, and likely will only be populated with a few naturally-spawned **[Envoys](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/mobs/envoy/)** when you first arrive. 

Many rooms of the Palace are guarded by inanimate **[Calcified Sentinels](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/mobs/calcified-sentinel/)**.

## Spawning Location
Palaces will generate above the following biomes:
* [Highlands](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/biomes/highlands/)
* [Highlands Forest](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/biomes/highlands-forest/)

## Inhabitants
There are a large number of [Calcified Sentinels](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/mobs/calcified-sentinel/) throughout the structure. This is the only place they can be found.

[Quints](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/mobs/quint/) will spawn from Monster Spawners found in this structure.
