## Function
Warded Jars are utility items that can be used to pick up and transport Allays and Quints more easily than other methods. 

Allays become much easier to transport and move to enclosed spaces where they can be used, and **[Quints](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/mobs/quint/)** can be moved to intentionally enlighten **[Calcified Sentinels](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/mobs/calcified-sentinel/)** or **[Envoys](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/mobs/envoy/)** without hassle.

## Obtaining
A Levitation Wand is crafted with 2 sticks and 1 Levita Bead which can be obtained by mining Levita ore rarely found in all Paradise biomes.

Levitation Wands are rarely dropped as loot as rewards from the Trial Spawners in **[Birdcage](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/structures/birdcage/)** structures.
