## Function
Paradise Lost adds two new variants of vanilla Flower Pots and Decorated Pots. They function identically to their Vanilla counterparts just with a different appearance.


## Obtaining

### Looting

Calcite Flower Pots and Decorated Pots generate in several structures in Paradise, including the **[Vault](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/structures/vault/)**, **[Remains](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/structures/remains/)**, and **[Palace](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/structures/palace/)**

The new Paradise-exclusive sherds "Coo" and "Sol" can be found via Archeology in the **[Remains](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/structures/remains/)**, or rarely in loot chests within the **[Palace](/wiki/paradise-lost/2.4.x/structures/birdcage/)**

### Crafting

Calcite Flower Pots are crafted like normal Flower Pots, but using 3 Calcite blocks instead of 3 Bricks.

</br>

Calcite Decorated Pots are crafted like normal Decorated Pots, but use Calcite blocks instead of bricks for blank sides, and must have a Calcite block in the center of the recipe. 

Any sherds Vanilla or from Paradise can be used and depending on if the Calcite block is in the center of the recipe will be crafted into a Calcite or normal Decorated Pot, with sherds being colored to match the pot material.
