## Description
The Remains are common structures that spawn scattered on the surface of Higlands Biomes. The structure is usually comprised of a large central temple surrounded by smaller buildings. Throughout the buildings and in random patches of the ground **Suspicious Paradise Dirt** can be found. Through Archeology, relics of the civilization can be discovered including exclusive **[Pottery Sherds](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/blocks/calcite-pottery/)** and the experience-saving **[Circlet of Knowledge](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/items/circlet-of-knowledge/)**.

## Spawning Location
Birdcages can spawn in the following biomes:
* [Highlands](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/biomes/highlands/)
* [Highlands Forest](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/biomes/highlands-forest/)
* [Highlands Thicket](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/biomes/highlands-thicket/)
* [Highlands Grand Glade](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/biomes/highlands-grand-glade/)

## Loot

### Archeology
Brushing **Suspicious Paradise Dirt** will drop one of the following:
* Calcite Block
* Calcite Flower Pot
* Sol Pottery Sherd
* Coo Pottery Sherd
* Circlet of Knowledge
* Stone Pickaxe
* Stone Shovel

### Decorated Pot Contents
Breaking any [Calcite Decorated Pots](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/blocks/calcite-pottery/) found in this structure can drop:
* **1-3** Flaxweave (common)
* **3-9** Flax Thread (common)
* **2-3** Flaxseed (uncommon)
* **1** Olvite (uncommon)
* **1** Levita Bead (rare)
