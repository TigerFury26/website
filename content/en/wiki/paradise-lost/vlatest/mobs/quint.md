## Behavior
Quints float around aimlessly until they spot a host, either an **[Envoy](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/mobs/envoy/)** or a **[Calcified Sentinel](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/mobs/calcified-sentinel/)**. When they spot a host, they will dart towards them and **Enlighten** them. For details on **Enlightenment**, check the respective page for the host.

Quints can be picked up and transported with **[Warded Jars](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/items/warded-jar/)**.

## Spawn Locations
Quints do not spawn naturally on their own.

Special Spawn Locations:
* **[Vault](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/structures/vault/)**: Will emerge when the **Enlightened** [Envoys](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/mobs/envoy/) that guard the structure are killed
* **[Palace](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/structures/palace/)**: Spawn from Monster Spawners within the Olvite Obelisks
* Will emerge from rare [Envoys](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/mobs/envoy/) that randomly spawn **Enlightened**

## Drops
Quints do not drop any items.
