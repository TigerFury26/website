Both forms of Sentinel guarding a mysterious room in a Palace structure.

## Behavior
Calcified Sentinels do not spawn naturally, and only generate inside of **[Palace](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/structures/palace/)** structures as inanimate statues. Each Sentinel wields a **[Bloomed Blade](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/items/bloomed-blade/)**.

### Enlightenment
When a **[Quint](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/mobs/quint/)** is nearby a Calcified Sentinel, it may choose to **Enlighten** it to bring the inanimate statue to life. While **Enlightened**, Sentinels walk slowly and do not recieve knockback from attacks. When killed, the Sentinel will release the [Quint](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/mobs/quint/) which may choose another host if not killed quickly.

## Spawn Locations
Calcified Sentinels do not spawn naturally, and only generate inside of **[Palace](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/structures/palace/)** structures as inanimate statues. They do not respawn in the structure.

## Drops
When killed, Calcified Sentinels will drop 2-5 **[Bloomed Calcite](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/blocks/bloomed-calcite/)** blocks, and have a 1/8 chance to drop their **[Bloomed Blade](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/items/bloomed-blade/)**.
