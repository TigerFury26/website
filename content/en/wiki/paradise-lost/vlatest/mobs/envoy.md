## Behavior
Envoys spawn throughout Paradise in dark places and during the night. Outside of structures they wield no weapons, wear no armor, and spawn in small groups of 1-2. Like other skeleton variants they burn up in the day and are afraid of wolves.

### Enlightenment
When a **[Quint](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/mobs/quint/)** is nearby an Envoy, it may choose to **Enlighten** the Envoy to empower it. While **Enlightened**, an Envoy will take half damage from all sources. They are easily distinguished from normal Envoys by their glowing eyes and particle effects. When killed, the Envoy will release the [Quint](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/mobs/quint/) which may choose another host if not killed quickly.

Envoys will rarely be Enlightened when they spawn outside of structures, and release a [Quint](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/mobs/quint/) when killed like normal.

## Spawn Locations
Envoys can be found in all Paradise Biomes and many structures.

Special Spawn Locations:
* **[Vault](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/structures/vault/)**: Spawns Enlightened with Glazed Gold Armor and Sword in each treasure room
* **[Birdcage](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/structures/birdcage/)**: Spawn from Trial Spawners with Olvite Armor and Weapons

## Drops
When killed, Envoys will drop 0-2 Bones and have a 1/20 chance to drop a **[Bloomed Calcite](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/blocks/bloomed-calcite/)** block.
