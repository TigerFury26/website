## Function
Bloomed Blades are sword weapons with no other special features than scaling damage via kills. Any normal sword or generic enchantments can be applied as normal.

### Collecting Souls
The Bloomed Blade starts at 3.5 Damage when dropped by a **[Calcified Sentinel](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/mobs/calcified-sentinel/)**, but will deal an additional 0.25 damage for every unique mob (passive, neutral, or hostile) slain with the blade, with no maximum besides the number of killable mobs in the game.

## Obtaining
Bloomed Blades have a 1/8 chance to drop from animated [Calcified Sentinels](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/mobs/calcified-sentinel/), who can only be found in **[Palace](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/structures/palace/)** structures.
