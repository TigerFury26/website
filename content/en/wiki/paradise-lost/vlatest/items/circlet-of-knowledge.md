Both a charged and uncharged Circlet of Knowledge in the central ruin of a Remains structure.

## Function
The Circlet of Knowledge can be worn in the helmet slot and upon player death will store that player's experience in itself rather than dropping on the ground. All experience is preserved rather than the usual 7 level limit. When charged from a player death, equipping it again will give that player the contained experience and discharge the Circlet.

Circlets of Knowledge can be enchanted with Unbreaking and Mending.

## Obtaining
Circlets of Knowledge are rarely found via archeology in the **Suspicious Paradise Dirt** blocks around and inside the ruins of **[Remains](/wiki/paradise-lost/vlatest/structures/remains/)** structures.
